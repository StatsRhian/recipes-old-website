---
title: Spiced Butternut Squash
layout: default
---

* 600g butternut squash
* 1/4 tsp turmeric
* 1 bay leaf
* 150ml water
* 1 small onion
* 1 clove garlic
* 50g butter
* 1 tsp cumin seeds
* 1 tsp coriander seeds
* 1 inch ginger

Squash, turmeric, bay leaf and water in pan. Lid on 20 min. Mash.
Cook rest separately. Add to squash.
Serve with rice.
