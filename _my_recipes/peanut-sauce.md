---
title: Peanut Sauce
layout: default
---

* 140g smooth peanut sauce
* 1 clove of garlic
* 1 cm of ginger
* Juice of 1 lime
* 1 tsp tamari soy
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 1 tsp sunflower oil
* 1/2 tsp brown sugar
* Drop of tabasco

Blend/chop ginger and garlic. Mix everything except peanut butter.
Add peanut butter spoon at a time, adding water if needed. Seal in glass jar and shake.
