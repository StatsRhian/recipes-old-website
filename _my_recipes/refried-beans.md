---
title: Refried beans
layout: default
---

* 1 onion
* 1-3 cloves garlic
* 2 tins beans pinto or kidney (250g dried)
* 1 tsp cumin powder
* 1/4 tsp corriander powder
* 150ml stock
* 2 tbsp fresh corriander 

Fry onion garlic in oil
Add spices, beans, stock. 
Cook until soft (10-20 min) and mash.
